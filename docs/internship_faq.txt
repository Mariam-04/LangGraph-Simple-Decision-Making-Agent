Internship Program Guidelines:

- Interns must complete a minimum of 6 weeks of training to receive a certificate.
- Interns are expected to work a minimum of 30 hours per week.
- Leaves must be approved in advance by the supervisor.
- Any violation of code of conduct may result in dismissal from the internship.
- Stipend will only be paid to those who submit weekly progress reports on time.
- Interns can request a letter of recommendation based on their performance.
